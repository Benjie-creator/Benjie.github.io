About Mother Kevin Junior School

Our History

Mother Kevin Junior School was started in 2003 by the Little Sisters of St Francis as a nursery school. It was established to provide quality education to underprivileged learners in our locality. Over the years, it has grown to offer primary education from Nursery to Primary 7, focusing on academic excellence and moral development.

Our Purpose

The school exists to nurture knowledgeable and productive children who will contribute to the development of their families, community, and country. We emphasize discipline, Christian values, and holistic growth in every learner.
